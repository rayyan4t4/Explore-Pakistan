    Explore Pakistan — Interactive Tourism Map

    Pakistan is a country of extraordinary diversity, home to some of the world's most spectacular landscapes and richest history. From the towering peaks of the Karakoram and Himalaya ranges in Gilgit-Baltistan to the ancient ruins of Mohenjo-daro in Sindh, from the lush green valleys of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa to the golden beaches of Balochistan, Pakistan offers countless experiences for every type of traveller.

    Tourist Places in Gilgit-Baltistan

    Gilgit-Baltistan is the adventure capital of Pakistan, featuring K2 Base Camp, Hunza Valley, Attabad Lake, Skardu, Shigar Fort, Deosai National Park, Rakaposhi Base Camp, and many more breathtaking destinations.

    Historical Places in Punjab

    Punjab is home to magnificent Mughal heritage including Lahore Fort, Badshahi Mosque, Shalimar Gardens (UNESCO World Heritage Sites), Rohtas Fort, Taxila archaeological sites, and Anarkali Bazaar.

    Tourist Spots in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa

    KPK features stunning valleys like Swat Valley, Naran, Kaghan, Malam Jabba, Chitral, and Tirich Mir, along with ancient Buddhist sites at Takht-i-Bahi.

    Places to Visit in Sindh

    Sindh is host to the ancient Indus Valley Civilization sites of Mohenjo-daro, the historic Makli Necropolis, Karachi beaches, Keenjhar Lake, and the spiritual Sehwan Sharif shrine.

    Tourism in Balochistan

    Balochistan offers pristine beaches along the Makran Coastal Highway, Ziarat Residency, Quetta, Hingol National Park, Princess of Hope, and the ancient city of Sibi.

    Azad Kashmir Tourism

    Azad Kashmir features Neelum Valley, Muzaffarabad, Rawalakot, Banjosa Lake, Shounter Lake, Sharda ruins, and the scenic Leepa Valley.
